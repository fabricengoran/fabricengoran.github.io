Our Story

Respect. Integrity. Pride in a job well done. These are the core values upon which Nobert Ngoran founded DHD Constancy in 2011.

A native of Baba 1, Nobert has a degree in Civil Engineering from the Advance School of Public Works, Cameroon. He headed multi-million dollar projects in Douala City, Yaounde and Bamenda, Kumba, before returning to Douala, where he managed major renovation and expansion projects for the his company.

Today, Nobert combines experience and knowledge with a commitment to quality and honest communication to do the best job possible for every DHD client.
